Encore

Story Summary

Encore is a coming-of-age story adapted from the real experiences of LeBaor. It follows Clinton, a junior high student navigating emotional turmoil, centered around his relationships with Laura and Fina.

Through first love, misunderstandings, and painful separations, Clinton gradually confronts his doubts, romantic ideals, and social insecurities. The story unfolds in both A and B narrative directions, concluding with reflections from adult Clinton in therapy, underscoring that “regret may remain, but understanding brings peace.”

Plot Structure

  • Infatuation: Clinton silently falls for Laura, his deskmate, with a budding relationship during and after the pandemic.
  • Confession & Dating: He confesses with friends' encouragement; they date sweetly until authority and self-doubt ruin the bond.
  • Breakup: Parental pressure and Clinton’s insecurity cause the split. He spirals, clings to games, and briefly gets close to Fina.
  • Fina's Role: A lively girl from a rough crowd. Despite affection, Clinton can't commit. Violence and misunderstandings follow.
  • Final Choice: In the B version, Clinton rejects Fina and tries to reconnect with Laura. The ending is open and bittersweet.

Character Highlights

Clinton: Sensitive, insecure, creative but conflict-avoidant. His arc shows personal growth through loss.

Laura: Gentle but passive. She’s a symbol of fragile first love crushed by reality and pressure.

Fina: Bold, passionate, yet lost. A mirror to Clinton’s confusion and emotional immaturity.

Others: Maika (quiet admirer of Laura), Mr. Green (authority), Dr. Ford (voice of reflection).

Artistic & Cultural Elements

  • Game scenes (Genshin, Sky) as metaphors for connection and distance
  • Jay Chou lyrics evoking nostalgic, bittersweet moods
  • Regional dialects (Sichuan, Chongqing) adding realism and voice

故事概述

《Encore》是一部青春成长小说，以作者LeBaor的真实经历为蓝本，讲述了初中生克林顿在感情与成长中的挣扎与觉醒。

故事围绕与劳拉和菲娜的两段情感展开，通过“初恋”“分离”“纠缠”与“选择”，呈现一个少年在理想与现实中挣扎的心理轨迹。

人物分析

克林顿： 敏感浪漫，内心多疑。最终在心理咨询中意识到，遗憾的背后是年少的不成熟。

劳拉： 安静羞涩，渴望被理解但屈服于现实压力。

菲娜： 热烈主动，实则缺爱。她是青春期“混乱”的具象化。
